500 Hour Kundalini Yoga Teacher Training in Rishikesh, India

Kundalini, the union of Shiva and Shakti. We awaken our inner sleeping energy, as Kundalini (Shakti) starts to rise our potential becomes limitless, abundance comes into our lives. Negativity, anxiety, stress, and fear begin to dissipate, allowing us to live our lives, rather than survive. Then we Become, our true selves. The ancient seers and sages of India perceived the spine as the tree of life, and safely practiced and taught the rise of the inner energy.

How Do I Become A Kundalini Yoga Teacher?

Here in Rishikesh at our world-famous Kundalini yoga ashram. We are the first Kundalini Yoga Ashram in Rishikesh. We have been teaching and training students over the last ten year both internationally and also within India. We have trained and supported over 10,000 students worldwide.

Kundalini Yoga teacher training in India is without a doubt the most valuable journey you will take. Our testimonials confirm our expertise in the matter and we are rated as one of the best Yoga Ashrams in India. Why wait to make this journey home, this will be the most important journey of your life join our course today… take that leap of faith.. and become…this course will be life-changing

Without a doubt, Kundalini Yoga is gaining so much popularity across the world that more and more job openings for skilled and certified Kundalini yoga trainers are on the list of requirements for various organizations. Amid the lush greenery of Rishikesh, you will spend time at our ashram where you will be taught our world-famous 500 -hour Kundalini yoga teacher training and discover the new, powerful you.

What topics are covered in our 500 Hour Kundalini Yoga teacher training in Rishikesh?

  •   A different perspective on life
  •   Cultivating a disciplined regular routine to foster a healthy way of life
  •   Enhancing and use of practices like self-respect and respect for others, forgiveness and letting go, connecting to the Divine, through the love of self.
  •   The ability to lead and guide others towards the divine yogic journey
  •   Whilst you are at the ashram you will follow a yogic lifestyle.
  •   Staying in a peaceful part of Rishikesh, near enough to walk to the local markets, famous bridges but far enough from the hustle and bustle to maintain the inner peace, surrounded by lush jungles at the back of the ashram, encased from all directions with the mighty Himalayan mountains and the flow of the Ganga right on our doorstep.
  •  You will be a Kundalini yoga teacher training with certification by Yoga Alliance under which you can obtain an international and professional qualification to teach yoga worldwide
  •   An opportunity for adventure sports such as trekking, mountain climbing, river rafting and more should you require
  •  Excursions to Ganga Arti, Kunjapuri, and Viashaista Caves.
  •  You will be looked after by teachers with a combined experience of teaching yoga over 20 years both internationally and on home soil.

We offer an immersive journey into the yogic concepts while evolving not only the teacher in you but also allowing you to find your inner self. Join us.. have no expectations, be vulnerable, allow the transformation to come to you.

Inclusion & Exclusions With This Course

Inclusions

  •   All classes
  •   Initiation and closing ceremony
  •   An arrival course kit, including a cleansing and detox kit, books, and pen
  •   Free Wifi-Internet facilities
  •   3 Vegetarian Yogic meals (except on Sunday)
  •   Detox tea
  •   Nature Excursions
  •   Book and study materials
  •   Himalayan Sunrise Tour
  •   Kunjapuridevi temple visit
  •   One Mala
  •   Meditation on Ganga beach
  •   Ganga Bath
  •   Kundalini meditation in an ancient cave

What is not included in the course fees

  •   Any expenses related to visa and travel.
  •   Private accommodation- $300 additional.
  •   Air-conditioner or a room heater- $150 additional
  •   Any external costs, charity donations, food/beverages outside the ashram

Requirements / Suitability

  •   People wanting to develop the skill of teaching in the path of traditional kundalini yoga and meditation
  •   Beginners through to advanced yoga practitioners
  •   Those wanting to awaken the hidden energy.
  •   The ability to lead and guide others towards the divine yogic journey
  •   Those with at least 6 months experience in practicing yoga or those with 200/300-hour certificates from other schools.
  •   Those who are seeking a 500hr yoga alliance certificate after completion of the course
  •   Those who are interested in teaching meditation kriya and kundalini practices & an open mind

    • Notes

  •   The booking amount of $200 is Non- refundable, the remaining fee is paid on arrival.
  •   If you would prefer private accommodation, there will be an additional cost of $300
  •   If you require air conditioning there is an additional cost of $150..
  •   The TTC starts on the 1st of every month and ends on the 29th of each month. Time allocated for self-study and practice time will be calculated in the total hours.
  •   It is an intensive program, so you will not have time to study any other courses alongside this one.
  •   The daily schedule may change according to weather and situations.
